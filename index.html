About Me

Domingos serves as a property tax director and in-house counsel at The Wolff Company, responsible for advising TWC's development, asset management, investment management and preferred equity platforms on state and local tax issues, and providing legal counsel to Revel Communities, a leading luxury senior independent living platform.

Before The Wolff Company, Domingos counseled REITs, private equity firms, and real estate developers and operators on state and local tax compliance and tax reduction strategies. He started his career advising the Maricopa County Assessor's Office through the 2008 financial crisis.

Active in policymaking, Domingos has worked with the Arizona Legislature on property tax issues, including leading an effort to curtail the authority of local county assessors to sue taxpayers.

Domingos volunteers at the Higley Unified School District, advising on budget override and bond proposals and teaching enrichment programs in the District's gifted program, and recently founded Flourish Living, a non-profit organization committed to resident flourishing and housing affordability.
